How Hubstaff’s payroll and time tracking software works

  1. Your team tracks time or adds work hours manually

    Have your team get the Hubstaff time tracking app or track time from their browser. Create projects and to-dos and assign them so everyone can track time.

  2. Add pay rates and frequency for each team member

    Set up payroll once and streamline almost everything. Add separate bill and pay rates for each team member of your business, then choose the payment frequency and method of payment.

  3. Team members are paid automatically or manually

    Hubstaff sends automatic payments to local and international employees based on employee hours tracked with the time clock, set rates, and frequency. You can also send manual payments at any time.
